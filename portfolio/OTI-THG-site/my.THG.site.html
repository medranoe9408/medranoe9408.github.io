The Hunger Games

Welcome to my website for the popular movie trilogy The Hunger Games

The Hunger Games is a trilogy based off of the trilogy written my Suzanne Collins. The movies are based around the districts of Panem during the 74th- 76th Hunger Games. In this futuristic, dystopian society, the capital draws two names from each district, one boy and one girl, to fight against in the Hunger Games. The 74th annual district 12 girl tribute is Katniss Everdeen. She is the protagonist and we follow her throughout the trilogy. Her male counterpart from district 12, Peeta Mellark, and Katniss are forced to test themselves, mentally, physically, and emotionally, to make it through the games.

The Hunger Games touches on many contraversal topics such as:

  1. Poverty
  2. Starvation
  3. Oppression
  4. The effects of war
  5. Social Patriarchy
  6. Gender Norms
  7. Alcoholism
  8. Power
  9. violence
and much more.
