The Hunger Games: Catching Fire




Release Date: November 20, 2013

Summary: After Katniss and Peeta win the 74th annual Hunger Games, they get an inside look of the capital and how it works. They are particularly disinterested in how it works, and spark a rebellion in the Districts of Panem. Katniss is faced with a struggle: fighting against the government, or fleeing with nothing. President Snow coerces Katniss into competing in the 75th annual Hunger Games with Peeta, dispite having already won and going against trained killers from the other districts. President Snow has a particular dislike for Katniss because he sees the effect she has on the rebellious side of the Districts of Panem.
